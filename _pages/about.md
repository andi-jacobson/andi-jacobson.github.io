I am a third year PhD candidate in both the Fessler and fMRI labs at the University of Michigan. I study combined diffusion relaxometry MRI, with a goal to find in vivo metrics to assess Alzheimer’s disease and related disorders white matter lesions. 

Outside of my academics, I enjoy aerial gymnastics (sling), rock climbing, piano, spending time with my pets. I enjoy leading community service projects focused on empowering and tutoring children from disadvantaged backgrounds (abuse survivors, previously homeless, etc.).
